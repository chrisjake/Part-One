#Adrenergic Drugs {#id}

Adrenergic drugs:
* Act via:
 * Dopamine (D)
 * Adrenoreceptors (α and β)
* Can be:
 * **Direct-acting**  
 Stimulate the receptor.
 * **Indirect-acting**  
 Stimulate the release of noradrenaline to cause effects.
* Classified as either:
 * **Naturally-occurring catecholamines**
 * **Synthetic catecholamines**
